WHO WE ARE

WELCOME TO SUN RAYS FOUNDATION

Sunrays Foundation(SRF), is a Humanitarian non-profit organization established in 2019 by a group of South Sudanese Youth. SRF was founded with the aim of supporting the vulnerable in the community with the key goal of providing sustainable community-based solutions aimed towards strengthening resilience among the most marginalized populations in South Sudan

Our Mission

To promote Inclusive social-economic empowerment and right-based participation of the most vulnerable and marginalized children, youth, women, and people with disabilities for sustainable.
