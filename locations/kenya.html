SRFembraces various methodologies aimed at ensuring quality delivery of our programmes. We continuously integrate new approaches based on the needs of our community and the current world trends. All our interventions have been selected based on an identification process of community needs and done through a participatory approach. We work closely with the government to strengthen already existing projects thus reaching more people and ensuring sustainability of the gains made. A continuous monitoring and evaluation processes is carried out to inform decision making and accountability. We are also keen on embracing proven models for work in our various programs.
